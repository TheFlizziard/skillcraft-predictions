The website where you can know what your true rank is, based on the SkillCraft dataset
SkillCraft is a dataset composed of 3,395 rows (each row representing one unique player) and 21 columns representing players stats (each column represents a different statistic). Each player (or row) is assigned to its league rank (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Master, Grand Master and Professional).
