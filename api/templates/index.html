{% block title %} Welcome to SkillCraft predictions {% endblock %}

The website where you can know what your true rank is, based on the SkillCraft dataset

SkillCraft is a dataset composed of 3,395 rows (each row representing one unique player) and 21 columns representing players stats (each column represents a different statistic). Each player (or row) is assigned to its league rank (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Master, Grand Master and Professional).
You can find more information on the dataset here

What is StarCraft ?

StarCraft II is a science fiction real-time strategy video game developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment. It is a very in-depth and complicated game. All the complexity resides in managing time, space and resources. Therefore, StarCraft II is considered as one of the hardest game to master. 

How to use SkillCraft predictions ?

First, you need to precise which statistics you want to use, you have to choose bewteen:
  • Using all the features available on the skillcraft dataset
  • Using only important features (APM, SelectByHotkeys, AssignToHotkeys, ActionLatency and GapBetweenPACs)

Then, you need to know what type of prediction you want, we provide two types of predictions:
  • Exact rank prediction
  • Group prediction
Exact rank prediction will predict your rank as it is defined in StarCraft II (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Master, Grand Master and Professional).


Group prediction will predict your group out of 4 (novice, competent, expert, pro). Groups are ranks we have defined, based on the existing ranks and on our knowledge on video games.


Then, all you have to do is enter your in-game statistics and click on the predict button.
A Machine Learning model will then give you your predicted rank or group with its precision.
